ABOUT US

House of Safety Wear & Farm Tools is a trusted supplier of high-quality farming equipment and protective gear in Uganda. Located in the heart of Kampala at Container Village, Planet Plaza Shop A14, we are committed to making farming safer and easier for everyone.

  • Wide variety of farming tools like hoes, pangas, slashers, and taplines
  • Safety gear including gloves, gum boots, bee suits, and chest warders
  • Competitive prices for both retailers and wholesalers

We pride ourselves on reliability, affordability, and countrywide delivery. House of Safety Wear & Farm Tools is your go-to partner whether you're a seasoned farmer or just starting out.

Motto, Vision, and Mission for House of Safety Wear & Farm Tools

Your Partner for Easy Farming!

To empower farmers with the best tools and safety gear, ensuring a safe and productive farming experience across Uganda. We aim to be the leading provider of farm equipment and protective gear, setting high standards in quality and accessibility.

To provide a wide variety of high-quality farming tools and safety wear, including hand hoes, pangas, gum boots, slashers, and bee suits, while offering competitive prices and countrywide delivery. We strive to support both commercial and non-commercial farmers, making farming safer and more efficient through reliable products and excellent customer service.
